What is H I R A Y A ?



Hiraya —an ancient Tagalog word—is a deeper term for sana or wish. The full phrase is Hiraya Manawari (Baybayin Hedaya Manawade ) which figuratively means "reach your dreams." Manawari means matupad/mangyari . Literally, the phrase means sana matupad/mangyari or "(I) wish it could happen." It connotes an inclination to pine for the impossible amidst hopeless situations.

This blog site serves as a medium to discuss timely political issues in the Philippines. Hiraya is the voice of young writers who "wish" to reach hundreds or even thousands of Filipinos through their opinions and thoughts about the mainstream political developments.

#SanaAll is the official hashtag that collects the sentiments of the people who share a common goal to raise awareness about the prevailing political issues.

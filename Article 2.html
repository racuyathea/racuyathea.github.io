An Article on People Choosing
Incompetent Candidates

By: Bryan James A. Pidazo | 2019 May 12

With the upcoming Midterm Senatorial Elections just around the corner, social media netizens are unstoppable as soon as they start posting, sharing, and commenting on the hottest and latest issues in the political arena. For instance, several universities were roasted by the netizens for meddling in the political affairs of the country.
While almost all of the law schools or student governments of the different universities conducted mock senatorial elections, the University of the Philippines, Saint Louis University, and San Beda University specifically received the most backlash from the netizens.
To sum up the results, majority of the population from all universities voted for the candidates of the Otso Diretso with Chel Diokno, Samira Gutoc, and Bam Aquino on the top (Lucas, 2019).
